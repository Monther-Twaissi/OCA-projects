MDM MANAGEMENT

MDM is your favorite management website to manage your work, it has the basic features anyone would need, our website is special becuase of its attractive and simple design, we provide you with the ability to change the colors of the background between the green color theme or the gray color theme, change the font size of the text bigger or smaller, and lastley change the font family to what you find comfortable to your eyes. From the features we have is a calculator with many operations like summaition, subtraction ,factorial ,division ,power and absolute to perform of any numbers you want, also we have a very simple and easy to use to do list to add the important tasks you don't want forget, and you have the ability to delete tasks one by one if you don't want a specific task to appear on your to do list anymore, you can also check the task to indicate that you completed that task, and if you open your completed tasks tab you'll see all the tasks you've completed.
